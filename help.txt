📈 UNDERSTANDING THE AIO PRICE CURVE
==================================================
💰 $600–$800 → BEST VALUE ZONE
This is where most buyers should focus. You typically get SSD storage, modern processors, and enough RAM for school, work, streaming, browsing, and everyday multitasking.

⚡ $900–$1,300 → PERFORMANCE SWEET SPOT
This range adds stronger processors, better webcams/microphones, nicer speakers, improved displays, and more premium build quality.

🔥 $1,500–$2,500+ → DIMINISHING RETURNS
Here, you mostly pay for premium materials, creator-specific display quality, design prestige, and niche features. Great for specific pros, but not always better value for normal users.
